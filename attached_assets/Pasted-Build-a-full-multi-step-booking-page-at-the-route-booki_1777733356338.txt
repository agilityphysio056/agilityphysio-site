Build a full multi-step booking page at the route /bookings for Agility Physio. 
This page must be smooth, modern, engaging and mobile-responsive. 
It should match our existing website colour scheme exactly:
- Primary: Teal/green-blue (#1a9b8c or closest match to agilityphysio.net)
- Background: Clean white (#ffffff)
- Text: Deep navy (#1a2e44)
- Accent: Soft teal highlight on hover/active states
- Font: Match existing website font (check current CSS for font-family)

The booking flow has 5 steps shown as a clean progress bar at the top:
Step 1 → Step 2 → Step 3 → Step 4 → Step 5

---

STEP 1 — SELECT CLINIC
Show 2 large clickable cards side by side (stack on mobile):
- Card 1: "Stanmore Clinic" with address, small map thumbnail, and a tick icon when selected
- Card 2: "Stockwell Clinic" with address, small map thumbnail, and a tick icon when selected
When a card is clicked it gets a teal border highlight and "Selected" badge. 
A "Next →" button appears below once one is selected.

---

STEP 2 — SELECT CLINICIAN
Show clinician profile cards for the selected clinic (2 clinicians for Stanmore, 
use placeholder data: Name, HCPC registration, photo placeholder with initials avatar, 
short 2-line bio, specialisms as small pill badges e.g. "Back Pain", "Sports Injury").
Cards are horizontally laid out, teal border on selection. "Next →" button appears on selection.

---

STEP 3 — SELECT DATE & TIME
Layout: Three-column layout on desktop, stacked on mobile.
- LEFT COLUMN: Available time slots — only appear once a date is hovered or clicked. 
  Show slots as clean pill buttons (e.g. 9:00am, 9:30am, 10:00am...). 
  Selected slot gets teal fill. Column header: "Available Times"
- CENTRE COLUMN (dominant): A clean inline calendar. 
  Current month shown. Available dates are bold/teal, unavailable are grey/strikethrough.
  Hovering a date previews times in the left column instantly.
  Clicking a date confirms it and locks the left column to show real slots.
- RIGHT COLUMN: Booking summary card — shows Clinic, Clinician, Date, Time selected so far.
  Updates live as user picks. This becomes the "Your Booking" receipt.

"Next →" only activates once both date AND time are selected.

---

STEP 4 — PATIENT DETAILS
Clean form with the following fields:
- First Name, Last Name (side by side)
- Date of Birth
- Email Address
- Mobile Number
- "Is this your first visit?" — Yes / No toggle
- Reason for appointment (dropdown: Back Pain, Neck Pain, Shoulder Pain, 
  Sports Injury, Post-Surgery Rehab, Other)
- Special notes / additional info (optional textarea)
- Checkbox: "I agree to the cancellation policy" (required)

Form validation inline — red border + message on missing required fields.
"Confirm Booking →" CTA button in teal, full width, bold.

---

STEP 5 — CONFIRMATION PAGE (Thank You Page)
Route: /bookings/confirmation
Large animated green tick icon (CSS animation, draws itself in).
Headline: "You're all booked in! 🎉"
Subtext: "A confirmation has been sent to [patient email].

Show full booking summary card:
- Clinic name + address

Two CTA buttons:
- "Add to Google Calendar" (generates .ics link)
- "Back to Homepage" → links to agilityphysio.net

    'value': 1.0,
    'currency': 'GBP'
  });
Add a comment // REPLACE AW-CONVERSION_ID and CONVERSION_LABEL with values from Google Ads

  window.dataLayer.push({ 'event': 'booking_confirmed' });
